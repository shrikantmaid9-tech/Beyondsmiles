Service 01

Dental Implants

Missing teeth affect both function and confidence. Our digitally-planned dental implants replace tooth roots with biocompatible titanium posts, topped with natural-looking crowns — giving you a permanent, stable solution that looks and feels like your own teeth.

  • Single-tooth, multiple-tooth & full-mouth implants
  • Digital implant planning for precision placement
  • High success rate with 1000+ implants placed

Root Canal Treatment

Save your natural tooth with our single-sitting, pain-free root canal therapy. Using rotary endodontic instruments and digital diagnostics, we treat infected or damaged pulp with precision — relieving pain fast and preserving your tooth for the long term.

  • Single-sitting RCT with rotary technology
  • Pain-free procedure with modern anesthesia
  • Crown placement to restore full function

FAQs

Common Questions

Is dental implant treatment painful?+

No. Implants are placed under local anesthesia with modern painless protocols, so patients typically feel little to no discomfort during the procedure.

How long does a root canal take?+

Most root canal treatments are completed in a single sitting using rotary endodontic technology, reducing multiple visits.

What orthodontic options are available?+

We offer traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligners depending on your needs and budget.

How long does teeth whitening last?+

In-clinic whitening results typically last 6 months to 2 years depending on diet and oral hygiene habits.

Do you treat children at Beyond Smiles?+

Yes, we offer gentle, friendly pediatric dentistry designed to make children comfortable with dental visits.
