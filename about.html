A Career Devoted to Precision & Comfort

Dr. Stanny Michael Kedari completed his BDS before pursuing a PGDAQUFE (Post Graduate Diploma) in France — a specialization that sharpened his focus on advanced implantology, aesthetic dentistry, and quality-driven clinical protocols rarely seen at neighborhood clinics.

At Beyond Smiles, he has placed over 1000 dental implants and treated more than 500 patients, combining European clinical standards with a warm, transparent approach that puts patients first — from the first consultation to the final follow-up.
